Giswater

GISwater is an open-source software project designed for water utilities and professionals working in the water industry. It combines the capabilities of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and hydraulic modeling to support the management and analysis of water distribution systems.
The main purpose of GISwater is to provide a comprehensive solution for the design, analysis, and optimization of water supply networks. It allows users to create and manage digital maps of water distribution infrastructure, such as pipes, valves, pumps, and reservoirs, within a GIS environment. These maps can then be used to perform various tasks, including network analysis, system modeling, and simulation.

My role on the project is to develop database architecture, create new functionalities using PL/SQL functions and triggers, migrate data from diffrent data sources into Giswater database and provide support to the end users.
